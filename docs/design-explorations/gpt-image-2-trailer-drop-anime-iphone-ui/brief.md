# WakeSaga GPT-image-2 Anime iPhone UI Exploration

## Product Brief

WakeSaga is an AI alarm and morning motivation app that turns each day into a personal training arc. This pass responds to the previous anime concept being too dense: keep the anime identity, but make the screens feel like clean, shippable iPhone app UI.

## Design Direction

**Anime iPhone UI**: native-feeling iOS product design with one controlled anime focal element per screen. The flow should feel like a modern alarm/social app first, with anime protagonist, rival, episode, and wake-card language as the emotional layer.

## Visual Notes

- Use iPhone-native spacing, safe areas, large tap targets, and clear bottom CTAs.
- Limit each screen to 3–4 primary zones.
- Keep text large and sparse.
- Anime identity should come through one hero illustration/avatar/card, not dense manga panels.
- Avoid poster collage, RPG HUDs, tiny thumbnails, overloaded badges, fake Japanese text, and fake social proof.

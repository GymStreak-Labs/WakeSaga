# WakeSaga GPT-image-2 Anime Trailer Drop Exploration

## Product Brief

WakeSaga is an AI alarm and morning motivation app that turns each day into a personal training arc. This pass keeps the "Trailer Drop" structure from the prior exploration, but makes the visual identity anime-native: every morning is framed like the opening episode of the user's own original series.

## Design Direction

**Anime Trailer Drop**: viral Gen Z social app structure wrapped in original anime opening energy. The app should feel like a personal episode premiere, alarm utility, and collectible character-card system, not a generic productivity dashboard or fantasy RPG.

## Visual Notes

- Keep the product functional: alarm time, mission, obstacle, narrator, wake gate, episode player, and CTAs must be clear.
- Use anime identity through composition, cel-shaded character silhouettes, manga cut-ins, speed lines, episode-title cards, expression stickers, and collectible cards.
- Keep the Gen Z layer through chunky prompt cards, friendly social-app surfaces, share/save controls, and screenshot-able receipt moments.
- Avoid copyrighted anime IP, fake Japanese text, unreadable tiny labels, all-purple AI gradients, and heavy RPG HUD chrome.

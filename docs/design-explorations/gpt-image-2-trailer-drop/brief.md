# WakeSaga GPT-image-2 Trailer Drop Exploration

## Product Brief

WakeSaga is an AI alarm and morning motivation app that turns each day into a personal training arc. This exploration treats every morning like a personalized episode premiere: the user sets up tomorrow's trailer before bed, wakes to a short AI audio jolt, clears a wake gate, then unlocks a Morning Episode and collectible share card.

## Design Direction

**Trailer Drop**: editorial streaming-app energy meets viral Gen Z self-improvement. The app should feel like a personal trailer studio, alarm utility, and daily artifact generator rather than a generic productivity dashboard, meditation app, or fantasy RPG.

## Visual Notes

- Use bold editorial hierarchy with tactile controls.
- Keep the product functional first: alarm time, audio state, wake gate, episode player, and CTA hierarchy must be clear.
- Make the social layer come from shareable cards/posters, not fake likes or social proof.
- Avoid copyrighted anime IP, fake Japanese text, generic neon AI gradients, fantasy HUDs, and bland meditation aesthetics.

# WakeSaga Onboarding Design Language

Source direction: GPT-image-2 concept `03-morning-persona-builder.png`.

## Core Idea

Onboarding should feel like building a morning persona, not filling out a productivity quiz.

The user is not "configuring an alarm." They are creating the character sheet for Episode 001:

- wake persona
- training arc
- morning rival
- narrator
- wake gate
- wake receipt

## Visual System

- **Surface:** warm paper studio, not dark RPG HUD.
- **Hero motif:** large anime avatar medallion with a small edit/action badge.
- **Interaction shape:** chunky stacked cards with color tabs and big readable labels.
- **Social layer:** "friends say" strip and tiny reaction tags, used as atmosphere rather than core navigation.
- **Anime identity:** original character energy, sunrise stickers, episode labels, brush marks, training-arc language.
- **Viral payoff:** the setup culminates in a shareable Wake Receipt.

## Voice

Use sentence-case, fast, social copy:

- "Pick your wake persona"
- "What steals your first 10 minutes?"
- "Name your morning rival"
- "Build my arc"
- "main character energy"

Avoid:

- generic "setup complete" language
- dark fantasy/RPG inventory terms
- fake Japanese text
- copied Trip/BFF mechanics
- over-explaining the product inside cards

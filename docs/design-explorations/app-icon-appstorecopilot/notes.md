## AppStoreCopilot vs prior Nano Banana read

The AppStoreCopilot route is more polished and commercial, especially for glossy/3D icon options. The earlier direct Nano Banana board had stronger raw Ink-and-Signal/anime restraint. I would combine them: use AppStoreCopilot polish on the simpler alarm+dawn mark.
